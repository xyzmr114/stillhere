36 million Americans live alone.

Most of them have no plan for what happens if something goes wrong.

29%of US households are single-person — a record high
79%of Gen Z report feeling lonely
10%of people who die alone aren't found for over a month
Gen Z doesn't know their neighbors. A 2023 Pew survey found only 1 in 4 young adults know even one neighbor by name. The informal safety net that used to exist — someone noticing your lights haven't been on — is gone.
