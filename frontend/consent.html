SMS Consent

Last updated: April 2026

Your consent

By providing your phone number and enabling SMS notifications, you consent to receive SMS messages from Still Here at the mobile number you provide. These messages include:

  • Daily check-in reminders at your scheduled time
  • Missed check-in alerts if you do not respond within your grace period
  • Account verification and security messages

You may revoke this consent at any time by disabling SMS notifications in your account settings or by replying STOP to any message.

Emergency contact messages

If you miss a check-in and your grace period expires, Still Here may send SMS alerts to the emergency contacts you have configured. Your contacts are not required to consent to receive these messages — you authorize Still Here to contact them on your behalf as part of the safety check-in service.

Emergency contacts can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message they receive.

Message frequency

Still Here sends a maximum of 1–2 SMS messages per day to your phone number:

  • One daily check-in reminder at your scheduled time
  • One occasional alert if a check-in is missed (after your grace period expires)

Emergency contacts will only receive messages when you miss a check-in.

Message and data rates

Standard messaging rates may apply to all SMS messages sent by Still Here, depending on your mobile carrier and plan. Still Here does not charge for SMS messages — any costs are from your carrier.

How to opt out

You can stop receiving SMS messages from Still Here at any time by either:

  • Disabling SMS notifications in your account settings
  • Replying STOP to any message you receive from Still Here

After opting out, you will receive no further SMS messages unless you re-enable SMS notifications in your settings. Replying HELP to any message will return support information.

SMS provider

Still Here uses Twilio as its SMS messaging provider. Twilio processes and delivers all SMS messages on behalf of Still Here. Your phone number and message content are transmitted to Twilio solely for the purpose of delivering your notifications.

Consent is not required

Providing your phone number and consenting to SMS notifications is entirely optional. Still Here can be used without SMS — check-in reminders and alerts can be delivered via email or push notification instead. Consent to receive SMS is not a condition of purchasing or using any Still Here service. Self-hosted deployments of Still Here are free and do not require SMS consent.
