Check in every day

One tap from a push notification, email, or SMS. Takes two seconds. That's the whole thing, most days.

02

Miss it — grace period starts

We don't panic. You chose your own grace window when you signed up. We wait. No alerts yet.

03

We alert your people

All of your emergency contacts, simultaneously. SMS, email, and push. Not one of them — all of them.

Grace period expired
04

Majority must confirm

One contact saying "she's fine" isn't enough. We require a majority vote before standing down. Then you double-confirm.

Accountability built in

Last resort: welfare check

Non-emergency line only. Never 911. Only if push ignored + grace expired + all contacts alerted + majority couldn't confirm.

Self-host it.

Still Here is fully open source and self-hostable. Run it on your own server with Docker Compose. Use your own push provider, email server, and database.

📦
Docker Compose
One command to run the full stack — API, worker, scheduler, Redis.
🔔
No Firebase required
Use Web Push with your own VAPID keys — no Google account needed.
📧
SMTP or Resend
Bring your own mail server or use Resend. Same emails, your infrastructure.
🔒
Private access via Netcore
Give contacts encrypted P2P access to your instance — no open ports needed.
